About

Yeun Soo Choi is a Communication Design and HCI researcher at Yonsei University's Graduate School of Communication & Arts. Her practice sits at the intersection of interaction design systems, human–robot interaction, and critical data visualization — with a focus on how designed interfaces mediate trust, agency, and meaning between people and intelligent systems.

Her work has been presented at international venues in the ACM CHI and UMAP research communities. Prior to graduate study, she worked in UX/UI design systems for technology companies across Seoul and San Francisco.
